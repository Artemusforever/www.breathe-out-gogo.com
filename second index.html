What is this project about?


This project is designed to provide support and promote mental health awareness for students that may be struggling with various challenges that can impact their academic and personal well-being. These challenges may include difficulty with their studies, such as academic struggles or learning disabilities, as well as other factors that can impact their mental, emotional, and physical health.

How to Prevent Mental Health Issues?

There are various ways to improve mental health, such as self-care and connecting with others. Self-care practices can maintain mental health and support the treatment and recovery of mental illnesses. Additionally, connecting with others, sharing our feelings, and knowing that others are there for us can significantly improve our mental health.

How to Prevent Mental Health Issues?

Studies have shown that talking about our problems and negative emotions with someone we trust can reduce stress, strengthen our immune system, and lessen physical and emotional distress. Writing about our problems is another way to release emotional pain and gain perspective. Psychologist James Pennebaker's research has found that writing about our emotional experiences can improve our mental and physical health. Keeping painful secrets increases stress and the risk of illness, while self-disclosure, whether spoken or written, relieves inhibition's long-term stress and leads to better health.

What are the goals of this project?

This project`s goal is to create a safe place for students to let out their worries without the fear of exposing themselves or being judged by their peers/society. Ultimately, the goal of this project is to help students thrive and strengthen them to keep going, even in the face of adversity. One of the primary goals is to improve the emotional state of students by letting them breathe out or release all their stress and emotions through talking about it or posting about it, without revealing their identity, in order to give them freedom and a safe place to relax.
